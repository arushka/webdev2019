Travel to Maui

Features

On the island of Maui, it's customary to play golf. The landscapes of this volcanic can be accurately described with the English expression “eye candy”: the soil of red color, covered with a bright green grass carpet, is emphasized by the deep blue of the ocean. Coupled with exotic vegetation and hotels, many of which are striking in their architecture, this creates an impressive effect.

Price

In Maui, travelers can expect to pay somewhere between $10 and $15 for breakfast, anywhere from $15 to $30 for lunch and considerably more for dinner. Depending on if you have cocktails, dinners in Maui typically cost around $30 to $50 per person
